About Slime Odyssey

Slime Odyssey is a collectible RPG where you hatch, collect, and battle with unique slimes across multiple realms. Build your dream team, tackle challenging bosses, join clans with friends, and become the ultimate slime master.

With over 10 unique slimes to collect and much more to come, 6 rarity tiers from Common to Mythic, and endless strategic possibilities, every battle tells a new story.
